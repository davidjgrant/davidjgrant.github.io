The app features easy navigation and an organised file system.

abc Dyslexia uses your phones camera to take pictures of large bodies of text, such as a newspaper, and automatically translating the font into a dyslexia safe font.

With multiple dyslexia safe fonts to choose from, you can then share the file to your email, notes or any note app on your phone!

The apps icons are familiar and easy to understand. But just in case, there is a tutorial in the settings to help you.
